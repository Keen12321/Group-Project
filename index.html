It was the late 60’s and Andrew Henderson was beginning his career as a private jet pilot. He flew all across the globe and met many exciting people. Approaching retirement age he was looking for a place to settle down and plant his roots. You see, Andrew was never the kind of man who ever dream of finding himself to lay his head. But here he found himself, old and grey, filled with wisdom and a sense of purpose to bring to new life into the world.

Andrew married Jane Dawson in 1975 and moved to a small town outside of Hartford, Connecticut. There they had a son named Henry Henderson.

Henry Henderson grew up to be a formidable track star in his high school. There, he met a young man named Jeff Gregory.

Jeff grew up learning how to cook with his adopted parents, who were both Hispanic. He loved cooking so much that he became a chef. After a successful career as a celebrity chef, Jeff decided that he wanted to settle down himself. But, not wanting to give up his passion, he opened a small restaurant business in Hawaii.
